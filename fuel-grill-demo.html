Our Story

BUILT ON BARTON STREET.

Fuel Grill is Hamilton's newest home for proper smash burgers. Every patty is pressed hard on a searing hot flat-top, building that caramelised crust that changes everything. We use locally sourced, free-run chicken — raised right, cooked fresh.

No frozen shortcuts. No corporate recipes. Just fast food done seriously, at a price that makes sense.
